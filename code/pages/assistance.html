Immediate Assistance

In urgent situations, knowing where to turn can save lives. Keep these critical contacts and steps at your fingertips:

  • Emergency Services (911): Call for police, fire, or medical emergencies—available 24/7 nationwide.
  • Poison Control: Dial 1-800-222-1222 for instant guidance on potential poison exposures.
  • Adult Protective Services: Report elder abuse or neglect to your state’s APS hotline—find your number online.
  • Crisis Hotlines: The Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988 provides free, confidential support for any mental health emergency.

Save these numbers in your phone and post them on your fridge or countertop for rapid access when every second counts.
