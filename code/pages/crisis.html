Crisis Intervention

When personal, financial, or mental health crises arise, timely support can make all the difference. Here’s where to find help:

  • Mental Health Helplines: Call 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline—free, confidential help for any emotional distress.
  • Counseling & Therapy: Search for sliding-scale or community mental health clinics in your area for affordable professional support.
  • Financial Aid Programs: Explore local nonprofits and agencies offering emergency rent, utility, and prescription assistance.
  • Peer Support Groups: Join organizations like NAMI or local support circles to share experiences and coping strategies.

Reaching out early can ease your burden—don’t hesitate to accept help from professionals or peers who care.
