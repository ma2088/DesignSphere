Online Safety & Privacy

Staying safe online is more important than ever. At Lumina, we empower you with the knowledge and tools to protect your personal information and digital presence. Whether you're shopping online, using social media, or managing your finances digitally, understanding privacy best practices can help you avoid scams, identity theft, and data misuse.

We offer practical guidance on how to create strong passwords, recognize phishing attempts, and safely connect to public Wi-Fi. You'll also learn how to manage privacy settings on your devices and favorite apps, so you stay in control of what you share and with whom.

Your online safety matters. Explore our tutorials, download tip sheets, or schedule a session with a Lumina privacy advisor to get started.
